# Product Launch Plan - Theme Sixteen

## AGID-Compliant Public Administration Theme

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Launch Date:** June 1, 2026 (Target)  
**Launch Type:** Major Release (v2.0 with Filament v5)  
**Owner:** Theme Product Team

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the comprehensive launch plan for Theme Sixteen v2.0, marking the transition to Filament v5 compatibility and the introduction of significant new features. The launch positions Theme Sixteen as the premier choice for Italian public administration websites requiring full AGID compliance with modern development tools.

### Launch Objectives

1. **Awareness:** Achieve 80% awareness among target PA technology decision-makers
2. **Adoption:** Secure 100 new installations within 90 days of launch
3. **Engagement:** Grow community to 500+ active members
4. **Validation:** Obtain 10+ public case studies from early adopters

## Risk Management

### Launch Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|-------|
| Critical bug discovered | Medium | High | Rollback plan, hotfix team | Tech Lead |
| Low initial adoption | Medium | Medium | Paid promotion boost | Marketing |
| Negative feedback | Low | High | Rapid response, transparency | Product |
| Infrastructure issues | Low | High | Redundancy, monitoring | DevOps |
| Team availability | Low | Medium | Backup assignments | Product |
| Press no-show | Medium | Low | Amplify owned channels | Marketing |

### Contingency Plans

#### Scenario 1: Critical Bug at Launch
1. Acknowledge issue publicly within 1 hour
2. Deploy rollback if severity is critical
3. Communicate timeline for fix
4. Deploy hotfix within 24 hours
5. Follow-up communication post-fix

#### Scenario 2: Low Registration for Webinar
1. Activate partner network for promotion
2. Increase social media frequency
3. Consider rescheduling if <20 registrants
4. Ensure recording for on-demand viewing

#### Scenario 3: Negative Community Reaction
1. Listen and acknowledge concerns
2. Respond transparently within 4 hours
3. Create action plan for valid feedback
4. Follow through on commitments
5. Update community on progress
