## 🔧 Standard Pattern Applied

### All Pages Have:
- ✅ Folio + Volt routing
- ✅ Single root element in `@volt`
- ✅ `<x-filament::icon>` for icons (Filament 5)
- ✅ `ui-brands.{name}` for social icons
- ✅ `heroicon-o-{name}` or `heroicon-s-{name}` for Heroicons
- ✅ Skip links for accessibility
- ✅ Header section
- ✅ Main content
- ✅ Footer section
- ✅ AGID compliant structure

### Icon Usage:
```blade
{{-- Social Icons --}}
<x-filament::icon icon="ui-brands.facebook" class="w-6 h-6" />

{{-- Heroicons --}}
<x-filament::icon icon="heroicon-o-arrow-right" class="w-6 h-6" />
<x-filament::icon icon="heroicon-s-star" class="w-6 h-6" />

{{-- Bootstrap Italia --}}
<svg class="icon icon-sm">
    <use href="#it-facebook"></use>
</svg>
```
